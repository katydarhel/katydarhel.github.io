Service RoutePlanner, provided by the company ThoriumSoft effectively calculates the route and schedule traffic, reduces transportation costs and increases the efficiency of the vehicle fleet.

Since the service RoutePlanner is a web application, all that is needed - is to connect to the Internet to address counterparty information about the fleet and order information - everything else the service will do for you. We have developed the most simple and user-friendly interface. Even complex operations are performed in "two clicks". Route calculation takes only 10-15 seconds. Therefore, you can easily plan graphs hydrating for many days to come.
